About the Artist

This section holds all of the business and personal information I feel like sharing, and some general website information, FAQs, and maybe something more.

whoami + F.A.Q.

My name is Alexander, I have a Bachelors of Fine Arts but my undergrad was almost entirely History and Art History. Started drawing/drafting in 2020 during COVID lockdowns.

In my free time I draw, collect books, and sometimes read them.

"What the hell is Realm & Empire?"

I don't know. I made it as a setting for a TTRPG my friends and I played in my moms basement, that was 15 years ago. It's based around RTS/MMO gaming experiences from the early 2000s mixed with history and fantasy. Now it is the flagship worldbuilding project I work on so I never have a moment of art block or burnout. Just pull stuff from the strange writings of my youth.
